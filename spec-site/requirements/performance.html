Requirements

  • PR-001
    Safety state publication rate shall be at least 10 Hz.
    allocated to: safety_monitor · verified by: TEST-SAF-010
  • PR-002
    Velocity command pipeline latency (raw → safe → motors) shall be at most 50 ms end-to-end.
    allocated to: safety_monitor, motor_driver · verified by: TEST-SAF-025
  • PR-003
    The rover shall travel at up to 0.7 m/s linearly and 1.5 rad/s angularly (iteration 1).
    allocated to: motor_driver · verified by: TEST-HAL-070
  • PR-004
    Visual SLAM pose estimate shall converge within 5 seconds of startup in a previously-mapped area.
    allocated to: RTAB-Map, ekf_global · verified by: TEST-LOC-020 (iter 2 — multi-session)
  • PR-005
    Object detection shall run at a minimum of 10 frames per second.
    allocated to: yolo_detector · verified by: TEST-PER-001
  • PR-006
    The mobile app shall display telemetry within 1 second of the rover's state change.
    allocated to: telemetry_publisher, mobile app · verified by: TEST-API-011
  • PR-007
    Rover battery life shall support at least 2 hours of continuous teleop operation.
    Operator-in-the-loop operation is the iteration-1 steady state; anything less makes testing impractical.
    allocated to: rover hardware design (4S 5 Ah pack, motor draw budget) · verified by: TEST-HAL-080 (field endurance)
  • PR-008
    Drone mapping mission shall complete within 3 minutes for a 0.25-acre yard.
    allocated to: drone_mission_coordinator · verified by: VRF-FIELD step 4
