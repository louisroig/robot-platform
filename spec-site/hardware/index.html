About this section

Hardware references are the single source of truth for physical wiring. When a pin assignment appears in a node SRS, a ROS parameter default, a deploy/udev/ rule, or a schematic, its canonical value lives here. When pins change, they change here first; the downstream artifacts follow. Do not fork.
