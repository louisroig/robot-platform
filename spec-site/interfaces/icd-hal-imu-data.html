Purpose

The /hal/imu/data topic carries 6-DoF inertial data (3-axis accelerometer, 3-axis gyroscope) plus a fused orientation from the IMU. It is consumed by the local EKF for orientation estimation and by the safety_monitor for tilt detection.

This is a safety-input interface: stale or missing IMU data is treated as worst-case tilt by the safety monitor.

Semantics

Axes follow REP-103: x forward, y left, z up. Gravity appears as +9.81 m/s² on z when rover is level.

Tilt is computed by the safety monitor as acos(gravity_z / |gravity|) using the orientation quaternion applied to the (0,0,1) gravity vector. Values exceeding tilt_warning_deg (18°) trigger WARNING; exceeding tilt_limit_deg (25°) trigger ESTOP.

Requirements

  • REQ-ICD-004-01
    Publication rate shall be 100 Hz ± 10 Hz during normal operation.
    verified by: TEST-HAL-020
  • REQ-ICD-004-02
    Orientation accuracy shall be < 2° pitch/roll after 10 s of stationary warmup.
    verified by: TEST-HAL-021
  • REQ-ICD-004-03
    The publisher shall cease publication rather than emit known-bad values when the sensor reports an I²C fault.
    verified by: TEST-HAL-022
