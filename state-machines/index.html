Why these are separate

A node's SRS describes what the node does; a state machine document describes how its behavior unfolds over time. Most nodes are stateless enough that their SRS suffices. The four documents here are for nodes where the state transitions carry safety or mission meaning on their own — the machine is not an implementation detail but part of the contract.

Each document pairs a drawn SVG diagram with a full transition table (source, target, trigger, guard, action), a list of per-state invariants, and timing constraints. Diagrams are hand-authored SVG — they are readable without opening Cameo.
