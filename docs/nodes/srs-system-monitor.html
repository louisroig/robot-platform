system_monitor

Aggregates per-node diagnostics, watches topic rates and publisher counts, and surfaces system-level health to the operator.

Document ID
SRS-HAL-004

0.1 · draft

platform_hal

Last Updated
2026-04-19
1.0

Purpose

Every custom node publishes diagnostics; system_monitor aggregates them, adds topic-rate monitoring and publisher-count audits, and produces a single health summary. Also enforces the rule "only one publisher on /hal/cmd_vel_safe" — detection triggers ESTOP.

2.0

Scope

In: topic-rate sampling, publisher enumeration, aggregate diagnostic publication, publication of a condensed health summary. Out: mission logic; individual node recovery actions.

3.0

Interfaces

Subscribers: all /diagnostics entries. Publishers: /diagnostics aggregate (under its own name), /system/health (platform_msgs/HealthSummary — TBD). Uses ROS 2 graph introspection for publisher enumeration.

4.0

Functional

  • SRS-HAL-004-F01
    Sample publisher counts on safety-critical topics (/hal/cmd_vel_safe, /safety/state) every 1 s.
    verified by: TEST-SAF-016
  • SRS-HAL-004-F02
    If more than one publisher is observed on either topic for more than 2 consecutive samples, emit a critical diagnostic that safety_monitor consumes to force ESTOP.
    verified by: TEST-SAF-016
  • SRS-HAL-004-F03
    Aggregate per-node /diagnostics entries into a condensed summary.
    verified by: TEST-HAL-060
5.0

Performance

CPU < 5%. Aggregation latency < 1 s.

6.0

Safety

  • SRS-HAL-004-S01
    Rogue publisher detection is the system's mechanism for enforcing "single authority" on safety topics. Detection must reach safety_monitor within 5 s of a rogue publisher appearing.
    maps to: SR-009
7.0

Dependencies

rclpy, diagnostic_msgs.

8.0

Configuration

ParameterDefault
publisher_count_poll_hz1
audited_topics[/hal/cmd_vel_safe, /safety/state]
aggregation_period_s1.0
9.0

Failure Modes

Own crash: systemd restart. Audit loss during restart: acceptable (a rogue publisher during a 2 s window will be caught on the next cycle).

10.0

Verification

TEST-SAF-016 (rogue publisher), TEST-HAL-060 (aggregation).

11.0
