motor_driver
Vendor-boundary node for two IBT-2 (BTS7960B) 43 A H-bridge modules driving the left and right tracks. Translates Twist commands into per-track signed duty on dual-PWM (RPWM/LPWM) channel pairs via GPIO, per HW-PI5-001. No encoder feedback; no battery telemetry. Above this layer the motor control is generic; below it, GPIO-level.
