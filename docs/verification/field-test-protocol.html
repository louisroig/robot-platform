M6 Field Test Protocol

End-to-end field procedure. The single pass/fail gate for iteration 1. Twelve steps from power-on to park-and-shutdown. Must pass on five consecutive days.

Document ID
VRF-FIELD
Method
Field
Automation
Manual (operator-driven)
Milestone
M6 · iteration 1 close
1.0

Purpose

Verify the full iteration-1 mission path works in a real yard with real hardware under operator control. This is the acceptance gate for iteration 1.

Requirements verified (primary): FR-001 through FR-010, SR-002 through SR-004, PR-008. Others indirectly exercised.

2.0

Setup

Environment
  • Clear daylight conditions. No precipitation. Wind < 5 m/s at ground level.
  • Yard size 0.1 – 0.5 acre. Reasonably flat (slopes < 15°).
  • No uninvolved persons in the test area (family, neighbors, or pets) during the drone flight phase.
Equipment
  • Rover assembled per ARCH-004, batteries charged.
  • Drone assembled, batteries charged, ELRS transmitter paired and functional.
  • Operator's phone with the mobile app; paired with a valid bearer token.
  • One additional person present acting as observer / fallback operator for the drone's manual controller.
Pre-test checks
  • Physical e-stop tested (press + release; motors confirmed cut then restored).
  • All systemd services reported green in /rover/state.
  • Safety state = OK.
  • Drone arms and disarms successfully on the bench.
3.0

Safety Briefing

  • The e-stop button is located [location per platform build]. If anything looks wrong, press it.
  • The drone has a manual-override transmitter. The observer holds it and can take over via mode switch at any time.
  • If the rover approaches a boundary and does not stop, press the e-stop.
  • If a person or pet enters the test area during flight, abort via app or drone controller.
4.0

Procedure

  1. Power up. Power on rover and drone. Wait 30 s for boot. Verify mobile app reports all systems green (safety = OK, battery ≥ 80%, all services reporting).
  2. Trigger mapping. Tap "Map My Yard" in app. The drone autonomously arms and takes off.
  3. Observe drone. The drone climbs to 30 m, hovers ~3 s while capturing, then descends and lands.
  4. Receive imagery. Within 60 s of landing, the aerial image appears in the app. [Verifies PR-008.]
  5. Draw outer boundary. User draws the outer polygon by tapping vertices on the image. App displays the polygon overlay.
  6. Add no-mow zone. User draws a no-mow exclusion around a tree or a feature. User saves the zone.
  7. Switch to teleop. User activates the saved zone. Switches the app to teleop mode. Rover responds to virtual-joystick commands within 1 s. [Verifies FR-005, FR-006.]
  8. Approach boundary. Drive the rover toward the outer boundary. Verify: the app shows a "boundary near" warning as the rover enters WARNING state (distance < 1 m).
  9. Cross boundary. Continue driving toward the boundary. Verify: the rover stops before crossing (or within 0.5 m if it crossed); app shows ESTOP with reason "geofence_violated". Reset via app after backing off. [Verifies FR-003, SR-004.]
  10. Step-in test. User steps in front of the rover during teleop. Verify: the rover stops within 500 ms; app reports ESTOP with reason "person_in_danger_radius". [Verifies SR-002.]
  11. Clear and resume. User steps back. After dwell, safety auto-clears. Resume teleop. Park the rover.
  12. Shutdown. Press physical e-stop. Verify: rover is inert. Close mobile app. Power down. Rosbag file closed; system_monitor final report written.
5.0

Abort Conditions

Abort the trial and record as FAIL if any of the following occur at any step:

  • The rover crosses the boundary by more than 1 m.
  • The rover fails to stop within 1 s of a person stepping into its path.
  • The physical e-stop fails to cut motor power (the safety-argument ground truth must hold).
  • The drone fails a flight mode transition and the observer must take manual control.
  • Any unexpected service crash that is not automatically recovered within 5 s.
  • Mobile app disconnects and cannot reconnect within 30 s.
6.0

Pass Criteria

Pass: All 12 steps complete without operator intervention (beyond normal teleop), and no abort condition occurs. The trial is logged as PASS.

Iteration 1 closure gate: 5 consecutive trial days PASS. A single abort resets the count.

7.0

Data Capture

  • rosbag2 recording throughout (automatic; system_monitor confirms).
  • Operator journal: one page per trial with wall-clock timestamps of each step, observed behavior, any anomalies.
  • Photo / video of the test site with boundary drawn (for documentation).
  • Mobile-app session ID recorded for later cross-referencing with bag data.
8.0

Post-Test

  • Review rosbag for any error-level diagnostics; cross-check with journal.
  • If any requirement was observed to fail, open a bug with trace to the relevant requirement.
  • After 5 consecutive PASS days, publish a closure report and bump iteration status to "M6 complete."
