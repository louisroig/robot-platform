1.0

Purpose

The /perception/obstacles topic complements /perception/people. It carries the non-living detections — chairs, benches, trash cans, vehicles, bicycles, and similar. In iteration 1 it is not a safety input; no ESTOP triggers from this topic. In iteration 2, the Nav2 costmap consumes it for obstacle avoidance.

3.0

Message

Type: platform_msgs/msg/DetectionArray (same as /perception/detections). Detections whose COCO class is not in the living-thing whitelist are published here.

4.0

Semantics

Iteration 1: purely informational. Displayed on the telemetry stream for operator awareness; recorded in rosbag for post-mission review. The rover does not react to obstacles published here.

Iteration 2: feeds the costmap via a simple inflation radius around each detection point. Classification-specific inflation (vehicle vs. cone) is deferred to iteration 3+.

5.0

Timing

Same rate as upstream, target 10 Hz.

6.0

QoS

RELIABLE (iter 1 for observability), KEEP_LAST depth 2, VOLATILE. May be relaxed to BEST_EFFORT in iter 2 when consumed by costmap (standard for sensor streams into costmaps).

7.0

Fault

Silent upstream produces silent topic; no independent fault modes beyond those on /perception/detections and the filter itself.
