/geofence/state

Rover position relative to the active zone polygon. Three states (INSIDE / WARNING / OUTSIDE) plus a continuous distance-to-edge measure.

Purpose

Published by geofence_enforcer at 10 Hz. Describes whether the rover's fused pose is inside, near the edge of, or outside the active zone polygon drawn by the operator. Consumed by safety_monitor for ESTOP decisions and by telemetry for app display.

Message

std_msgs/Header header uint8 state # 0=INSIDE, 1=WARNING (near edge), 2=OUTSIDE float32 distance_to_boundary_m # signed: positive = inside, negative = outside
4.0

Semantics

Distance is signed point-in-polygon distance using the active zone's outer polygon minus exclusion polygons.

  • INSIDE (0): distance_to_boundary_m ≥ warning_threshold_m (default 1.0 m).
  • WARNING (1): 0 < distance_to_boundary_m < warning_threshold_m. Rover is still inside but near the edge.
  • OUTSIDE (2): distance_to_boundary_m ≤ 0. Rover is outside the outer polygon or inside an exclusion polygon.

Transitions use 0.25 m hysteresis to avoid chatter at the edge.

When no zone is active (boundary_ingester has published nothing), the node publishes state=INSIDE and distance=+∞; safety_monitor treats this as "geofencing not enforced" rather than a fault.
