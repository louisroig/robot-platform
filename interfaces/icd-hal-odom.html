/hal/odom

Wheel odometry from the RoboClaw motor controller. Pose is integrated from track-side encoders using skid-steer kinematics.

1.0

Purpose

The /hal/odom topic carries wheel-encoder-derived odometry for the rover. The roboclaw_driver reads encoder counts from the motor controller, applies skid-steer kinematics, and integrates to produce a pose in the odom frame.

This is the primary proprioceptive sensor for the rover's local-frame EKF. It is fast (50 Hz), low-latency, and drifts over time — exactly the profile the EKF expects.

4.0

Semantics

The driver reads left and right track encoder counts, computes:

// Skid-steer forward kinematics (track_width = L) v_left = (ticks_left - last_ticks_left) × m_per_tick / dt v_right = (ticks_right - last_ticks_right) × m_per_tick / dt v = (v_left + v_right) / 2 // m/s ω = (v_right - v_left) / L // rad/s // Pose integration (midpoint method) θ += ω × dt x += v × cos(θ) × dt y += v × sin(θ) × dt

The odom frame is permitted to drift relative to map; this is the normal and expected odometry contract. The EKF consumes the twist (velocity) for fusion and largely ignores pose drift.

Fault Behavior

FaultResponse
RoboClaw unplugged / USB disconnectroboclaw_driver enters FAULT; publishes /hal/odom_status.encoders_healthy = false. No /hal/odom messages emitted.
Encoder wrapDriver uses signed 32-bit delta arithmetic. Single wrap is handled; two missed reads around wrap could corrupt — covered by TEST-HAL-004.
One encoder failsDetected by cross-check of commanded vs sensed velocity. Driver transitions to degraded; reports on /diagnostics; odom publishing continues with increased covariance on yaw.

Requirements

  • REQ-ICD-003-01
    The publisher shall emit Odometry at 50 Hz ± 5 Hz.
    verified by: TEST-HAL-003
  • REQ-ICD-003-02
    Covariance shall be populated using calibrated wheel-slip variance, not hard-coded zeros.
    verified by: TEST-HAL-004
  • REQ-ICD-003-03
    Absolute pose drift over a 30 m straight-line traverse shall be less than 1.5 m at default tire inflation.
    verified by: TEST-HAL-005 (field)
