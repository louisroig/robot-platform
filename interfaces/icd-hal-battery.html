Pack voltage, current, percentage, and health derived from the RoboClaw's battery sense input. Consumed by the safety monitor and system monitor.

Purpose

Published once per second. Reports pack voltage, current draw, estimated state-of-charge percentage, and a health flag. Used by safety_monitor to trigger CRITICAL state at low SoC and by system_monitor for diagnostics and telemetry.

4.0

Semantics

Voltage → SoC curve is chemistry-dependent. For 4S LiPo: 16.8 V = 100%, 14.8 V nominal (~60%), 12.0 V = 0%. For 4S LiFePO4: flatter curve, 14.6 V = 100%, 13.2 V nominal, 10.0 V = 0%. Curve selection via battery_chemistry parameter.

Percentage is low-pass filtered (τ = 5 s) to suppress transients during acceleration.

5.0

Timing

1 Hz ± 0.2 Hz. Poll cadence matches; no value publish when RoboClaw query fails.

6.0

QoS

RELIABLE, KEEP_LAST depth 3, TRANSIENT_LOCAL (so late-joining subscribers see a starting value immediately).
